About Me

My name is Asti !

I am a fullstack software developer aka software engineer aka frontend developer aka psychology & yoga expert

My previous experiences in creative, mental health, and customer service areas informs everything that I do, such as my ability to adapt quickly and stay motivated to find best possible solutions for challenging problems. I bring someone who is professional, progressive, and easy to work with. I am passionate about innovation, responsiveness, accessibility, and user experience. Currently I am working on multiple projects and serve as Instructor Associate for Software Engineering Immersive course in General Assembly school.

I came to the industry after a near death experience, I was tending a bar in Pacific Palisades and when I went up to a liqour room to restock the bar the gas tank fell on me and went off loudly spitting gas, and leaving me stranded in the tight space thinking I am without a doubt going to die underneath all those vodka bottles :) I was thinking about programming before but after this incident I finally completely changed my direction towards it and I have never looked back.

I have a background in psychology, political science, and international relations. In addition, I worked as a DJ for 12 years and as a yoga instructor. I did a lot of searching and inner work and here I am. Programming for me is a way for us to connect not only within our countries but also internationally, to get access to things we couldnt access before, and to erase the walls that we put or maybe that someone put for us. Coding is like music, like art - it does not see your nationality, your race, your religion, your language, or your gender. You are one with every single person who build things before you and who will continue building them after you are long gone. It is a beautiful thing.
